Personal Pronouns

Pronouns are words which can replace nouns that have been mentioned or implied in previous statements or questions. Pronouns can replace animate (living), inanimate (non-living), singular and plural nouns in sentences.

Se non si conosce la personal si dà del "voi", con Вы, sennò del tu con Ты.

  • Это Иван. Он журналист. - This is Ivan. He is a journalist.

  • Вот парк. Он слева. - This is a park. It is on the left.

  • Это Таня. Она студентка. This is Tanya. She is a student.

  • Это наша школа. Она справа. - This is our school. It is on the right.

  • Где письмо? - Вот - оно - Where is the letter? - Here it is.

  • I like you - Я тебя люблю

  • You talk to me - Вы мне говорите

  • You talk about me - Вы говорите обо мне

  • You talk with me - Вы говорите со мной

Notes

In russo non c'è l'articolo. Quindi, журналист means either "the journalist" or "a journalist"; письмо means either "the letter" or "a letter".

In Russian the verb "to be" is not used in the present tense. In English you would say: "He is a journalist"; "She is a student"; "Where is the letter? - Here it is", but in Russian you simply say: Он журналист; Она студентка; Где письмо? - Вот оно!.
