The adverb по-

The adverb по-рýсски is used when expressing that something is done in Russian. When adverbs use the hyphenated по-, the indicated meaning is in the ... faschion:

говори́те по-рýсски - to speak in Russian

This can be done for most foreign languages and cultures:

  • по-англи́йски  in English;

  • по-францýзски  in French;

  • по-неме́цки  in German;

  • по-украи́нски in Ukranian;

  • по-итальянски  in Italian;

  • по-япóнски  in Japanese;

Вы говори́те по-англи́йски? /vy ga-va-rí-tye pa-an-glíjs-ki?/ Do you speak English?
