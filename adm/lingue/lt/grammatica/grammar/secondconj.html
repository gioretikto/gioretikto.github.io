Second Active Conjugation

The Latin active second conjugation has a stem ending in ē- the infinitive ends in "-ēre". Verbs belonging to this conjugation are conjugated as the verb monĕo, "to warn, advise" whose principal parts are:

monĕo, mones, monŭi, monĭtum, monēre, 2nd tr., «to warn, advise»
