Greeks nouns of the second Declension

Greek nouns of the second declension often have double desinences, one of greek origin and the latin one. This generally occurs in the nominative and accusative singular, with the endings in -us (latin) / -os (greek), and in -um / -on respectively. Those in os are masculine, those in on are neuter.
