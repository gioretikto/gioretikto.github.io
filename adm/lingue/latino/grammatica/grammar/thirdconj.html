Third Conjugation - Active Voice

The Latin active second conjugation has a stem ending in ĕ, the infinitive ends in "ĕre". Verbs belonging to this conjugation are conjugated as the verb monĕo, "to warn, advise" whose principal parts are:

lĕgo, legis, lēgi, lectum, legĕre, v.tr., «to read»
