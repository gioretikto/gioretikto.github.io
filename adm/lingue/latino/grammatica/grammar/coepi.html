The defective verb coepi

The verb coepi, coepisti, coeptum, coepisse, «I have begun», derives from the perfect of coepio which has gone out of use. It is defective in the tenses of the present stem, which are integrated by the forms of the verb incipĭo, having similar meaning. From the perfect and supine stems derive the following forms:
