Numeral Adjectives

Numeral Adjectives are those used to express nummbers, In Latin thery are divided into four classes:

  • Cardinals: they express number simply like unus, one; duo, two, answering the question "quot?", «How Many?».

  • Ordinals: denote the place in a row , or series, as primus, irst, secundus, second; they answer the question "quotus?, quota?, quotum?", «In which position?».

  • Distributives: answering the question "quotēni?, quotēnae?, quotēna?", «How many to each?», as singuli, one by one, bini, two by two, or two to each.

  • Numeral Adverbs: answering the question "quotiens? (How many times?), as semel, one once; bis twice;
