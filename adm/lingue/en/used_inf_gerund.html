Used to

Used to + infinitive - Habitual action in the past

  • I used to smoke (but I don't anymore)

  • He used to eat meat (but he doesn't anymore)

  • We used to drive fast (but we don't anymore)

  • They used to be happy (but they aren't anymore)

  • She used to be healthy (but she isn't anymore)

  • He used to drink a lot (but he doesn't anymore)

  • Did you use to play tennis?

  • I didn't use to like cheese (but I do now)

To be used to + Noun or Gerund

  • I am used to getting up early.

  • I'm not used to starting work so early;

  • I'm not used to being around new people.

  • They are used to working quickly.

  • We are used to the teacher;

  • She is used to Canadian winters;

  • Are you used to living in Canada.
