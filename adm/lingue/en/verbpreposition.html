Verbs with preposition

In English, a verb is usually followed by one or more specific prepositions, Words cannot be conbined randomly. We say:

  • We go to a meeting

  • We arrive at a meeting

  • The mathematician worked to arrive at the answer.

  • We arrive in a city or country

  • Sania Mirza arrived on the Indian tennis scene in 2004

The phrasal verb is the combination of a verb and a preposition.
