Avverbio

Nouns can be either countable or uncountable. Countable nouns (or count nouns) are those that refer to something that can be counted. Nouns like coche (car) or manzana (apple) are countable. This means that indefinite articles and numbers can be used with them:

una casa- a house

dos cajas - two bozes

unos niños- some children

Nouns like aceite (oil) or agua (water) are uncountable. This means that it is usually not possible to use indefinite articles or numbers with them:

Hay leche en la cocina - There is (some) milk in the kitchen

No hay agua - There isn't (any) water
