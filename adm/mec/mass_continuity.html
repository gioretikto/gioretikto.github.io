Examining Fig.1, assume a flow of 6 litres per minute is running through Port C. The same quantity of liquid is the flowing through both chamber A and B, each of which are of equal volume

Exercise. At Section 1 of the circular pipe system shown in Fig.x, the velocity of the water is 2 m/s. Find the velocity at Section 2 and the discharge flow rate.

Ans. Since the density of water is constant, v1A1 = v2A2. Therefore

2 m/s ⋅ π (3/2)2 = v2 ⋅ π (1.5/2)2
v2 = 8 m/s
Discharge flow rate = 8 ⋅ π (0.015/2)2 = 0.0014 m3/s or 1.4 l/s ■
