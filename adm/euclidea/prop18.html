Proposizione I.18

Proposizione I.18 - In any triangle, the longer side subtends the larger angle.

Proof - Let ΔABC be a triangle with BC > AC. We must show that ∠CAB > ∠ABC. Choose D on BC so that CD ≌ AC. Note that ∠CAD is part of ∠CAB so that by CN5, ∠CAB > ∠CAD. By Pons Asinorum, ∠CAD ≌ ∠CDA so now we have ∠CAB > ∠CDA. Since ∠CDA is an exterior angle of the triangle ΔADB, by the Exterior Angle Theorem, ∠CDA > ∠ABC giving us ∠CAB > ∠ABC, as was to be shown.□
