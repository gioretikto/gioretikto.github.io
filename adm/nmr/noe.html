The Nuclear Overhauser Effect (NOE)

The Nuclear Overhauser effect (NOE) is a powerfull tool that can be used to enhance otherwise insensitive NMR spectroscopic signal, to make unambiguos stereochemical assigments, to explore site binding, and to elucidate tertiary and quternary structure in biomolecules.
