Break e continue

A break statement is used to terminate loop like for, while, do-while, and in the switch statement. break statement terminates the loop body and transfer control to the next statement after the loop.

continue statement causes the loop to continue with the next iterazione without executing remaining statements. It is used for for, while, do-while loops.
