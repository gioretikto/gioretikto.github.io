Proposizione I.10

Proposition I.10. A line segment can be bisected.

Proof Let AB be given. Construct ΔACB, an equilateral triangle on AB. Bisect ∠ACB and let the bisector intersect AB at the point D. We have AC ≌ BC, ∠ACD ≌ ∠BCD, and CD = CD so by SAS, ΔACD ≌ ΔBCD. As corresponding sides, AD ≌ BD .□
