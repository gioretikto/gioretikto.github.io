Next suppose that the theorem has been proven for sets with up to p - 1 vectors. Next, we are given {v1, ..., vp} and we construct {z1, ..., zp}. Notice that {z1, ..., zp-1} are obtained by applying the Gram-Schmidt process to {v1, ..., vp-1}, and thus by the induction assumption {z1, ..., zp-1} is a linearly independent set and holds for k = 1, ..., p -1.
