4.15.4 Definition. We call prime subfield or fundamental subfield, P of a field F the intersection of all subfields of F.

Since F is a field, there exists at least a subfield of F, and the intersection of an arbitrary number of subfields is never empty since every subfielf contains 0 and 1.
