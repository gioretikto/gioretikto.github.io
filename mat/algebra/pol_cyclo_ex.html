Exercise on cyclotomic polynomials

Exercise 1. Prove a) that the product of two nth roots of unity is again an nth root of unity, b) and the inverse of a nth root of unity is is a nth root of unity.

Solution. Indeed, if αn = 1 and βn = 1 , then (αβ)n = αnβn = 1. If (ζ)n = 1 taking the -1 power of both sides we get (1/ζ)n = 1.

Solution. It sufficies to consider ζn/d, with ζ primitive root of xn −1 because
